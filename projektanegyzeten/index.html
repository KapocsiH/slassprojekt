Popular Switch Types

Linear

Smooth keystroke without tactile bump or audible click. Popular for gaming.

Examples: Cherry MX Red, Gateron Yellow

Tactile

Provides a bump feedback without an audible click. Great for typing.

Examples: Cherry MX Brown, Zealios

Clicky

Offers both tactile feedback and an audible click. Satisfying but louder.

Examples: Cherry MX Blue, Box Jade

Keyboard Layouts

TKL (80%)
